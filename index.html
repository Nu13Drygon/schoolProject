Welcome to the Island of Taniti

Discover Taniti, a captivating tropical paradise in the heart of the Pacific. Spanning less than 500 square miles, this island holds a world of contrasts - from sandy to rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush rainforests, to a mountainous interior crowned by a gentle, active volcano. With an indigenous population of 20,000, Taniti's economy has shifted from fishing and agriculture to a growing tourism industry. Embrace the chance to explore its rich landscapes and culture, as Taniti unveils its authentic charm to the world.
